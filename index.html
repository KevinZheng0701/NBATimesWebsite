NBA Times is a website where you can get all the news about the NBA.


The National Basketball Association is a professional basketball league in North America. It is one of the major professional sports leagues in the United States and Canada and is considered as the top men basketball organization in the world. There are five major stats points in basketball: points, assists, rebounds, blocks, and steals. There are other substats like field goal percentage, three-pointer percentage, etc. Moreover, there are 30 teams with each team composing of a maximum of 15 players, totaling up to approximately 450 players active in the NBA. The NBA is found on June 6, 1946 in New York. Currently, it is the 77th season since the founding of the NBA.
